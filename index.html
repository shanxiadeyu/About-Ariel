About Me

A literate young man who doesn't want to make games is not a good creator.

The writing experience

After winning an award in a provincial essay competition in grade 6 of primary school, I followed my teacher in junior high school to systematically learn to write essays and participate in competitions at various levels, and I didn't stop until my senior high school exams. I won the second prize in the New Concept Composition Competition, the first prize in the Ye Shengtao Cup National Composition Competition, and so on. The awards are not enough to represent all of me, but if you are willing to know more.

Starting point for a game text planning

Entering Justice Online was my first step in trying to become a qualified game copywriter. In Justice I learnt the basics of being a game planner, and was responsible for implementing, packaging and writing content that went live one after another. At the same time, my internship period covered before and after the launch of the game, and I gained a deeper understanding of the intensity of the work in the game industry and the handling of online problems.

Opinion Insight

Instead of emphasising themselves, creators need to think more about ‘why should the public choose this game’. Based on this kind of thinking, I will try to create a game that is as close to the positioning of the product as possible.
